Unannounced Project: Island Builder

Project Overview

Unannounced project relying heavily on procedural generation to create believable towns and cityscapes from user-placed landmarks.

Players freely sculpt and mark up a heightmap, and the game then uses that data to generate an explorable series of interconnected island towns.

Role

Proc-gen Algorithm Developer

Project Details

Team: Undisclosed, Melbourne-based indie studio, in partnership with American publisher

Status: Ongoing as of 2021

Environment: Unity

Primary Tasks

Research:
- High-level design approach for procedural town generation given arbitrary terrain and landmarks
- Auditing and evaluation of existing approaches to proc-gen environments for viability
- Development of long-term roadmap for live-edited environment to regenerate in real time
- One-on-one liaison with creative director to accommodate changing project requirements

Development:
- Design and implementation of algorithms used in townscape generator (graph traversal, terrain gen, fluid simulation, edge detection, TSP optimisation solvers, etc)
- Generation of believable road networks between user-placed landmarks on an arbitrary terrain heightmap
- Generation of towns and built-up areas surrounding landmarks and road networks
- Generation of forests and vegetation, respecting built-up areas
